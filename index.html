A View from the top

Hello and welcome to our whole class blog on hiking!

We have just had a fun-filled day in the Brecon Becaons which included an exciting climb to the top of Pen-Y-Fan.

We had fun cimbing up to the top and looking at the view, which was amazing.

We also had a few funny moments like how a sheep was just blocking the path and bahhing at us, it was quite funny.

And once we got to the top, we stayed there for a little before heading back down. It was very fun!
